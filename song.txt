 
Þat mælti mín móðir,

at mér skyldi kaupa

fley ok fagrar árar,

fara á brott með víkingum,

standa upp í stafni,

stýra dýrum knerri,

halda svá til hafnar

hǫggva mann ok annan,

hǫggva mann ok annan.

My Mother Told Me
My mother told me,
myself I would buy
a vessel with strong oars,
sail afar with the Vikings,
stand upon the prow,
steer the noble ship,
set course out to sea,
hew one man and another,
hew one man and another.
